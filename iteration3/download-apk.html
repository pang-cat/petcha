    • Check the option Uknown Sources and Tap OK on the prompt message.
    • For newer Android versions (Oreo, Pie, Android 10, 11, and 12)
    • Go to your Android Settings > Apps & Notifications or just Apps and select Advanced or the three dots option at the top-right corner. (This option may vary depending on your device)
