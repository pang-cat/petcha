Your Productivity Companion

You never have to be alone in finishing your tasks ever again!
PetCha is here to accompany you through your daily duties, keeping
you motivated, engaged, and entertained while you're at it.

To-do List

Using PetCha as your To-Do list allows you to organize your daily tasks with ease.

Set your target dates and leave it up to PetCha to keep all your duties in one place.

Pet Simulation

You're not alone in doing your tasks. PetCha gives you your very own pet who will keep you company while you go through the list of things that you need to get done.

Make your pet happy by completing the tasks that you set!

Customization

Customize PetCha to fit it to your very own liking.

From different Purrrnitures to choose from and more decorations to unlock, you can make a warm and inviting home for your companion.

Achievements

As you work through your To-Do list, PetCha will reward you for your productivity.

Collect points as your achievements, by accomplishing tasks to unlock more decorations for your pet to enjoy.

Stay Productive

PetCha is your one-stop for all things to help you stay productive.

Equipped with a To-Do list and a pet of your own, PetCha will help you finish your tasks without the stress. All you need for a productive day is a list and a companion.

Progress without the Stress

PetCha focuses on keeping our users engaged and productive without
having to feel the stress of a compelling task.
