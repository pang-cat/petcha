About PetCha

Petcha is the new and intuitive way to help you achieve your goals with a companion every step of the way. PetCha aids in procrastination tendencies and stimulates creativity among users through customizable progress monitoring activities, learning companion features, and reward-giving mechanisms. Whether it's for academic purposes, employee tasks, or your everyday to-do,
PetCha is here!

Make a To-Do List

List down tasks that you need to accomplish for the day by tapping the "+" button. Every time you finish your task, you are also collecting food points. Remember to do your tasks daily and feed your pet to keep it healthy. Set a date for your tasks so you won't forget.

Equip Purrniture in PetDepot

Enjoy customizing a personal space for your own or for your pet! PetDepot is your in-store for your PetHome. To be able to unlock each tier level, you must accomplish a specific number of tasks. Tap the "Equip" button to place your purrniture and decorations. Subscribe to PetCha Premium for more tiers.

Unlock PetChievements

Increase motivation by rewarding yourself and receive a badge from PetCha. Each badge comes with big points which can help unlock decorations too. Continue to use the PetCha App and your hard work will definitely pay off!

Progress without the Stress

PetCha focuses on keeping our users engaged and productive without having to feel the stress of a compelling task.
